Biryani

Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, marinated chicken, and a blend of spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves. It is layered and slow-cooked to perfection, often garnished with fried onions, coriander, and boiled eggs. A popular dish in South Asia, it is known for its rich taste and fragrant appeal.

Rasmalai

Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a popular Indian dessert made of soft, spongy cottage cheese dumplings (rasgullas) soaked in sweet, flavored milk infused with cardamom and saffron. It is garnished with nuts like almonds and pistachios, offering a rich, creamy, and mildly sweet taste

Dal Bhaat Chokha

Dal Bhaat Chokha

Dal Bhat Chokha is a traditional meal from Bihar and eastern India. It consists of dal (lentil soup), bhat (steamed rice) , and chokha (a mashed dish made from roasted or boiled vegetables like potatoes, eggplant, and tomatoes, mixed with mustard oil and spices). It is a simple yet flavorful combination, often served with pickles and ghee.
